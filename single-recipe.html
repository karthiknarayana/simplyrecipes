Banana Pancakes

Whip up a batch of fluffy banana pancakes in no time! Simply mash a ripe banana, whisk in an egg, then fold in flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Cook the batter on a hot skillet until golden brown on both sides. Serve warm with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, sliced bananas, or berries for a delightful breakfast treat. Enjoy the sweet and savory combination that's sure to satisfy your cravings!

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Breakfast Pancakes Food

Instructions

Step 1

Mix 1 or 1/2 cups dry pancake mix with mashed ripe banana.

Step 2

Whisk in an egg and 1/2 cup flax seed meal until combined; gradually add 1 cup skim milk.

Step 3

Fold in flour mixture; cook on a skillet until golden brown.

Ingredients

1 or 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk

Tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
